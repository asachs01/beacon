# A better family command center

Beacon is an open-source, self-hosted family dashboard built for wall-mounted displays. It gives your family a single, glanceable view of the day — calendars, weather, and more — without subscriptions, cloud lock-in, or a $650 price tag.

Beacon runs as a **Home Assistant add-on**, so if you already have a smart home hub, you're most of the way there.

---

## What you get

### Calendar sync
Connect your Google Calendar (or any CalDAV/ICS source) and see everyone's schedule at a glance. Each family member gets their own color so you can tell events apart from across the room.

### Weather at a glance
Current conditions and forecast pulled directly from your Home Assistant weather entity. No extra API keys, no rate limits.

### Dark mode by default
Wall-mounted displays look best with dark backgrounds, especially in the evening. Beacon defaults to dark mode and can switch to light mode automatically during the day.

### Touch-first design
Big tap targets, smooth transitions, and a layout designed for fingers — not cursors. Works great on tablets and touchscreens of all sizes.

### Self-hosted and private
Your family's data stays on your network. No accounts to create, no cloud services to trust, no telemetry phoning home.

### Free forever
Beacon is open source under the MIT license. No subscriptions, no premium tiers, no "free trial" that expires. Just a family tool that works.
