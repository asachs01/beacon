# Weather at a Glance

**Duration:** ~40 seconds

---

## Scene 1: Dashboard Widget (0s–10s)

**Scene:** Dashboard view focusing on the weather widget — current temperature, condition icon (sunny, cloudy, etc.), high/low for the day.

**Voice:** "Weather is right on your dashboard — current temp, what it looks like outside, and today's high and low. No app to open, no searching. It's just there."

**Transitions:** Fade in to dashboard, gentle zoom toward weather widget.

---

## Scene 2: Full Forecast (10s–25s)

**Scene:** Navigate to the full weather/forecast view. Show a 5–7 day forecast with icons, highs, lows. Rain chances visible on a couple of days.

**Voice:** "Tap in for the full forecast. See the whole week ahead — looks like rain on Wednesday, so maybe move that barbecue. Each day shows the high, low, and conditions."

**Transitions:** Tap weather widget, view expands to full forecast.

---

## Scene 3: Calendar Integration (25s–40s)

**Scene:** Switch back to calendar view. Point out the weather icons embedded in the day column headers — sun on Monday, clouds on Tuesday, rain on Wednesday.

**Voice:** "And here's a nice touch — those weather icons show up right in your calendar too. When you're planning the week, you already know what the weather's doing. No second-guessing."

**Transitions:** Navigate to calendar, highlight day headers with weather icons, fade out.
