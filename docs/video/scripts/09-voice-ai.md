# Voice & AI Control

**Duration:** ~40 seconds

---

## Scene 1: Voice Commands (0s–15s)

**Scene:** Show a Home Assistant Assist voice interaction — either through the HA app or a voice satellite. "What's on the calendar today?" and Beacon data comes back as a response.

**Voice:** "If you run Home Assistant, Beacon works with Assist — HA's built-in voice system. Ask 'what's on the calendar today' and it reads back your schedule. Ask 'what's on the grocery list' and it tells you."

**Transitions:** Fade in to voice interaction demo. Show the query and response.

---

## Scene 2: AI Agent Tools (15s–30s)

**Scene:** Show a terminal or chat interface where an LLM agent (like Claude or ChatGPT with tools) is querying Beacon's MCP tools. "Add milk to the grocery list" — and it appears on the dashboard.

**Voice:** "For the AI-curious — Beacon exposes MCP tools that let language models interact with your family data. Your AI assistant can check the calendar, add to lists, or look up chores. It's like giving your AI agent a window into your household."

**Transitions:** Split screen — AI chat on one side, Beacon dashboard updating on the other.

---

## Scene 3: Natural Language (30s–40s)

**Scene:** Show one more example — "What chores does Emma have left today?" with a natural language response.

**Voice:** "It all works through natural language. No special commands to memorize. Just talk to it like you'd talk to a person."

**Transitions:** Response appears, hold, fade out.
