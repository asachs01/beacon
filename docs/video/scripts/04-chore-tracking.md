# Chore Tracking

**Duration:** ~45 seconds

---

## Scene 1: Family Members (0s–10s)

**Scene:** Chores view showing family member avatars/names across the top — Mom, Dad, Emma, Jake. Each person has assigned chores listed below them.

**Voice:** "Chore time. Every family member has their own list of jobs. You can see at a glance who's responsible for what."

**Transitions:** Fade in to the chores view.

---

## Scene 2: Checking Off Chores (10s–25s)

**Scene:** Tap to check off a chore — "Take out trash" gets marked done. Show the satisfying check animation. Then show another family member's chores with some already completed.

**Voice:** "When you finish a chore, check it off. There's something satisfying about watching that list get shorter. And you can see how everyone else is doing too — no more 'I already did mine' arguments."

**Transitions:** Check animation plays, scroll to next family member.

---

## Scene 3: Leaderboard (25s–45s)

**Scene:** Show the leaderboard or summary view — streaks, completion percentages, maybe earnings if configured. One kid has a 5-day streak.

**Voice:** "Here's the fun part — the leaderboard. Streaks for doing chores consistently, and if you set it up, you can tie chores to allowance. Five-day streak? That's worth bragging about at dinner."

**Transitions:** Scroll to leaderboard section, highlight streak badge, fade out.
