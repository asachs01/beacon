# Works on Any Screen

**Duration:** ~30 seconds

---

## Scene 1: Desktop View (0s–10s)

**Scene:** Beacon on a full desktop browser — wide layout, all sections visible, spacious design.

**Voice:** "Beacon looks great on a big screen. Wall-mounted tablet, old laptop in the kitchen, a monitor in the hallway — the layout stretches to use every pixel."

**Transitions:** Fade in to desktop view.

---

## Scene 2: Tablet View (10s–18s)

**Scene:** Resize or switch to a tablet-sized viewport. Layout adapts — maybe a two-column view, slightly more compact but still very usable.

**Voice:** "On a tablet, it tightens up nicely. Same features, just arranged to fit the smaller screen."

**Transitions:** Smooth resize animation or cut to tablet viewport.

---

## Scene 3: Phone View (18s–30s)

**Scene:** Switch to phone-sized viewport. Single-column layout, bottom navigation, touch-friendly buttons. Show a quick swipe between views.

**Voice:** "And on your phone, it's a full app. Swipe between views, check the calendar on the go, add something to the list from the grocery store. Same data everywhere — because it's all synced through your server."

**Transitions:** Phone viewport appears, show a swipe interaction, fade out.
