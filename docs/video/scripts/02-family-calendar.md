# Your Family's Calendar

**Duration:** ~45 seconds

---

## Scene 1: Week View (0s–15s)

**Scene:** Calendar in week view. Multiple color-coded calendars visible — Mom's in blue, Dad's in green, Kids' in orange. A mix of all-day events (birthdays, school holidays) and timed events (soccer at 4pm, dentist at 10am).

**Voice:** "Here's the calendar. Everyone's schedule, color-coded so you can tell at a glance whose thing is whose. All-day events sit up top — birthdays, school holidays. Timed stuff fills in below."

**Transitions:** Fade in directly to the calendar view.

---

## Scene 2: Weather + Filters (15s–35s)

**Scene:** Point out the weather icons on each day header. Then tap the filter pills at the top to toggle calendars on and off — show only Mom's calendar, then all calendars again.

**Voice:** "See those little weather icons on each day? You always know if soccer practice might get rained out. And these filter pills up here — tap one to show just that person's calendar. Tap again to bring everyone back."

**Transitions:** Highlight weather icons with a subtle zoom, then show filter interaction.

---

## Scene 3: Event Detail (35s–45s)

**Scene:** Tap on a calendar event to show the detail view — time, location, description.

**Voice:** "Tap any event for the details. Time, location, notes — it's all there. No more yelling across the house asking 'what time is the thing?'"

**Transitions:** Event detail slides up, hold for a beat, fade out.
