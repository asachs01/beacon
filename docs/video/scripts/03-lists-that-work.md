# Lists That Actually Work

**Duration:** ~40 seconds

---

## Scene 1: Grocery List (0s–15s)

**Scene:** The lists view with a grocery list open. 8–10 items visible — milk, eggs, bread, bananas, etc. Some items already checked off.

**Voice:** "Here's something every family needs — a shared grocery list. Add stuff from your phone, check it off at the store, and everyone sees the same list in real time."

**Transitions:** Fade in to the lists view with the grocery list selected.

---

## Scene 2: Checking Off Items (15s–25s)

**Scene:** Tap to check off a couple of items. The items get a strikethrough and move to the bottom or fade. Then tap the "add" button and type a new item.

**Voice:** "Check things off as you grab them. Need to add something? Just type it in. It shows up on every screen instantly."

**Transitions:** Show the check animation, then the add-item interaction.

---

## Scene 3: Switching Lists (25s–40s)

**Scene:** Switch from the grocery list to a different list — maybe "Packing List" or "House Projects." Show that each list has its own items.

**Voice:** "And you're not stuck with one list. Grocery list, packing list for vacation, house projects — switch between them with a tap. Whatever your family needs to keep track of, there's a list for it."

**Transitions:** List selector animation, switch to new list, hold briefly, fade out.
